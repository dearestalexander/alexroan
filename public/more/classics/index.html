Classic cars

In 2022 and 2023 I spent a year working on classic cars. I had the chance to take a break from consulting work and I used that time to buy cars and trucks at auction, do some basic repairs and sell them on. It was also an excuse to enjoy driving some of the cars I love.

Maybe I had been watching too many of those car garage reality tv shows.

But seriously, I believe there are a lot of good reasons to get into classic cars.

  • They have more aesthetic appeal than modern cars (i.e. they look great!)
  • You can experience a less diluted driving experience
  • Greater safety through fewer safety devices (which are shown to increase dangerous driving)
  • Enjoyment of the sounds and smells of old-school engines
  • Less reliance on computers
  • Easier self maintainance
  • Environmental benefits â€“ avoidance of disposal & the reductions in new car manufacturing.
